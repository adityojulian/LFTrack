LFTrack: Instant, Accurate, and Secure LFT Results at Your Fingertips

Transform your health monitoring with LFTrack, an accurate and user-friendly LFT (Lateral Flow Test) scanning app. Get instant results, track your history, and share reports with ease.

Key Features

📱 Real-Time LFT Scanning

Simply point your camera at your LFT and receive instant analysis and results. LFTrack employs a lightweight TFLite model to interpret test results accurately within milliseconds.

📊 Comprehensive History and Analysis

Keep a detailed record of your tests over time for better health monitoring and outcomes. LFTrack automatically uploads results to a secure Firebase database, ensuring data integrity and privacy.

📤 Export Results

Generate reports and export your data in CSV format for easy sharing with healthcare providers.

How LFTrack Works

  1. Scan Your LFT: Open LFTrack and use your phone's camera to scan your LFT.
  2. Review Results: Get immediate, clear results alongside guidance and advice.
  3. Track and Monitor: View your test history with detailed analytics.
  4. Export and Share: Easily export your data and share it with healthcare professional.

The Story Behind LFTrack

This Flutter-based cross-platforms mobile application is designed to streamline the way Lateral Flow Tests (LFTs) are processed and managed by forcing as minimum user interaction as possible while maintaining user-friendly interface and intuitiveness. The app, through integrated light model (TFLite) and data management technologies, enables users to scan LFTs using their smartphone cameras, automatically interpret the results, and seamlessly store these results in a secure database.

From the moment the LFT is positioned within the camera's frame, the app initiates a countdown, performs the scan, interprets the result, and stores it—all without unnecessary steps or inputs from the user.

Technical Highlights

LFTrack is at the forefront of technological innovation in health monitoring. Here’s a glimpse into the robust technical stack that powers the app:

🚀 Built with Flutter

Crafted using Flutter, Google's UI toolkit for building natively compiled applications for mobile, web, and desktop from a single codebase.

🧠 Powered by TensorFlow Lite

Utilizes TensorFlow Lite for efficient and accurate on-device prediction of LFT results, ensuring quick and reliable analysis.

🔍 Integrated with Google ML Kit

Incorporates Google ML Kit's barcode scanning technology for seamless decoding of LFT barcodes, enhancing user experience and accuracy.

🎨 Material You Design

Embraces Material 3 design principles for a fluid, adaptable, and personalized user interface that feels at home on any device.

🔒 Secured with Firebase Auth

Leverages Firebase Auth to provide a secure and hassle-free authentication experience, keeping user data protected.

💾 Database with Firestore

Employs Firestore for real-time database storage, enabling the app to store and sync data across users and devices effectively.

⚙️ State Management by GetX

Utilizes the GetX framework for efficient state management, enabling a reactive and stable performance throughout the app.
